Project manager

In this role, you will be expected to build up and develop an effective team of engi- neers, set up and streamline processes and ensure that each development stage is accomplished and each feature mission is achieved.

What will you do?

  • Oversee several features simultaneously
  • Ensure that production plans are fulfilled, estimations are met, the decisions made are consistent
  • Develop clear and transparent development plans
  • Manage project risks, analyze alternatives, ensure that the decisions agreed upon are implemented, help tackle complex project issues
  • Solve issues related to the interaction within/between teams and with other units and studios as well
  • Optimize and streamline development processes; introduce new practices to improve team efficiency
